This is a recording of Ed Felton\u2019s plenary session from AusCERT 2014.

Ed Felton is a professor of computer science and public affairs at Princeton\u2019s centre for information technology policy. From 2011 to 2012 he was the first Chief Technologist for the Federal Trade Commission. He\u2019s a very well known and highly regarded researcher and academic and he spoke at AusCERT on security in a surveilled world.
